STAC Courses for Fall 2018

This semester I am taking courses from four different programs.

Programs

  • Mathematics

    The mathematics program deals primarily with people who like numbers. Topics of study include algebra, calculus, and problem solving.

  • Statistics

    The statistics program is for people who like apply math to the world of data. Topics of study include regression, experimental design, and problem solving.

  • Data Science

    The data science program is for people who like data. Topics of study include machine learning, coding, and problem solving.

  • Film Studies

    The film studies program is for people who need to fulfill their last general education requirement but would rather be in another problem solving course. Topics of study include watching movies and talking about them afterwards.
